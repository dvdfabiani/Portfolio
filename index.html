Values

Understanding

    Understanding the business needs, unique views of the stakeholders, indicators to measure, and the end-user, is priority to start each project.

User-Centered

    Design placing the user at the center, proposing the best ways to help people achieve their goals.

Research

    From understanding the business, benchmark, always going through the user, knowing new technologies and how they affect the design, research is key in every process.

Equity focused

    Let’s go beyond inclusive design, seek to be equitable with users, thinking about the needs of one and that it works for all.

Discuss results

    Share test findings with stakeholders, make it known that decision-making does not come from the stomach, but the data.

Iterate

    It is part of the whole project, it is the opportunity for improvement that is found in each small instance.

We don't remember the product itself,
